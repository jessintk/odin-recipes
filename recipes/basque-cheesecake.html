Basque Cheesecake

Description

Healthy homemade banana bread made with the most simple indredients to satisfy your sweet tooth craving.

  • 100g coconut oil
  • 150g brown sugar
  • 1.5 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 188g all-purpose flour
  • 3 mashed banana
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 0.5 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1.5 tsp cinnamon
  • (optional) chopped buts/ 1 banana/ chia seeds
Steps
  1. mix all the wet ingredients
  2. mix all the dry ingredients
  3. combine all
  4. put into pan and add toppings
  5. Bake at 160C for 45-50 minutes
